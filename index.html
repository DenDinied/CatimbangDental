Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal Treatment

    For 2000 Pesos

    Root canal treatment is a dental procedure that involves removing infected or damaged pulp from the inside of a tooth.

  • Tooth Restoration

    Tooth restoration is a dental procedure that involves repairing or replacing damaged or missing teeth.

  • Dental Prophylaxis

    Teeth cleaning procedure that removes plaque and tartar, promoting oral health and preventing gum disease.

  • Tooth Whitening

    Tooth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that lightens the color of teeth, removing stains and discoloration.

  • Orthodontic Treatment

    Orthodontic treatment involves the use of braces or aligners to straighten and align teeth.

  • Tooth Extraction

    Tooth extraction is a dental procedure where a tooth is removed from its socket in the jawbone.
